Black Horse

Holo Museum series started with Ocean Dream. Ocean Dream was the result of a week long hackthon style development. I was in charge of writing, working on the timeline in Unity, finding the right music, and even doing voice over.

But it stole the show during GMIC. We had so many people waiting in line to try that we needed to borrow staff from other booths.



Go Further

After GMIC, Ocean Dream quickly expanded into the entire Holo Museum series and is the major product of Ximmerse now.
